## Explain Flow of $ARGUMENTS

Trace and explain the complete flow:

1. **Entry point** — where does it start?
2. **Data flow** — how does data move through the code?
3. **Dependencies** — what services or modules does it rely on?
4. **Output** — what does it return or produce?
5. **Edge cases** — what could go wrong?

Use simple language with a step-by-step breakdown.
Keep it beginner friendly but technically accurate.
